# Math-Race---Educational-Game
Math Race is the ultimate educational challenge where speed meets skill. Designed for learners of all ages, the game adapts to your skill level, ensuring that every player finds their "sweet spot" of challenge. Your speed on the track is powered by the speed of your calculations—the faster you solve, the faster you go!
Level,Focus Areas,Target Age,Gameplay Feel
Easy,Addition & Subtraction,Ages 5–8,Focuses on foundational fluency and building confidence with basic numbers.
Medium,Multiplication & Division,Ages 9–12,A fast-paced challenge designed to master times tables and mental division.
Hard,"Mixed Operations (+,−,×,÷) & Brackets",Ages 13+,High-level strategy requiring quick thinking and knowledge of PEMDAS/BODMAS.
